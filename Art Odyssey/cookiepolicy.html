Cookies Policy

WHY DO WE USE COOKIES ?
Cookies are essential for providing our services and enhancing your browsing experience. They help us understand your needs and preferences, improving the quality of our services.

HOW DO WE USE COLLECTED COOKIES ?
We only collect cookies when you visit our website. Collection may occur through interactions such as browsing, signing up, or engaging with our content. It is important for us to be able to track the necessary information for our services.

WHAT TYPE OF COOKIES DOES ARTHAUS IMPLEMENT ?
• Essential Cookies: Necessitiy for the functionality of the website.
• Performance Cookies: Used for analytics and improving site performance.
• Functionality Cookies: Enhance user experience by remembering preferences.
• Security Cookies: These cookies authenticate users, prevent login fraud, and safeguard user data from unauthorized access.

HOW DO WE HANDLE YOUR COOKIE DATA ?
Cookie data is shared only with user consent or as legally required. Stringent security measures are in place to protect cookie data. Users control cookie preferences through browser settings. Contact us for inquiries or access requests regarding cookie data. Third-party cookies may be used for advertising or analytics. Policy updates may occur periodically; please review regularly.

OUR CONTACT DETAILS

Should you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cookies Policy or the handling of your cookie data, please don't hesitate to contact us.
